My name is Danielle.

I am 26 years old.

I am an artist, and an engineer.

I was born in Washington, but I have lived in Oregon, California, and Nevada.

I work as a QA Lead at Spotlight Education, in Davis, CA.

Before coming to Spotlight, I worked in various jobs in retail and foodservice, mostly as a cashier and a lead.

Cashiering. S U C K S.

Moody customers. Call outs. Return policies. Shoplifters.

I did this while I was in college to get my bills paid. I worked nearly full time (try to find a retailer that will hire a full time, minimum wage employee), and was also a full time college student.

My entire life has led me to where I am today, and the path I've walked has created the person I have become.

I am immensely proud of my education.

I was raised by a family of laborers. I worked as a child.

We did landscaping. We did construction. We cleaned houses. We did what we had to.

We worked hard for everything we had.

Even now, I work hard for everything I have, and everything that I will have.

I have earned four degrees in the arts and in STEM.

I still strive to push forward in my education and in my career - I do this for my future, and for the future of the family I will have.
